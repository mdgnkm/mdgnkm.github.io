Additional Primitives

Truncated cylinder and cone primitives.

Anti-aliasing

Image pixels subdivided into an NxN grid.
Jittered sample of primary rays taken from each subpixel.

Refraction

Transmission rays obtained using Snell's law.
Index of refraction and transparency added as parameters to Materials.

Glossy Reflection

Random sample taken based on polar/azimuth angles of reflected ray.
Colour of sample rays weighted based on cosine probabilty distribution.

Depth of Field

Gridded aperture lens centered around each primary ray.
Sample rays from each grid cell aimed at focal point, where the primary ray hits the focal plane.
Focal distance and aperture diameter added as optional parameters to render command

Area Lights / Soft Shadows

Random sample rays taken from intersection point on a surface to area light.
Intensity from light rays weighted based on proximity to center of light source.

Toon Shading

Flat colours based on luminance.
Edges detected using surface normals / 3x3 Sobel filter.

Painterly Rendering

Based on Hertzmann's Painterly Rendering with Curved Brush Strokes of Multiple Sizes (1998).
